# Superstore Sales Analytics Project

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Superstore retail data to understand sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior across regions, categories, and time.

##  Objective
- Identify revenue and profit trends
- Analyze regional and category performance
- Evaluate impact of discounts on profitability
- Provide actionable business recommendations

## Tools Used
- Excel (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
- SQL (Data Analysis)
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

##  Dataset
Superstore sales dataset containing order, customer, product, and sales information.

##  Key Analysis Performed
- Total revenue and profit analysis
- Category and sub-category performance
- Region-wise profitability
- Monthly sales trends
- Top customers and products
- Discount vs profit analysis

## Dashboard Highlights
- KPI cards (Revenue, Profit, Profit Margin)
- Sales trend analysis
- Regional and category breakdown
- Interactive filters

## Business Insights
- The West region generates the highest revenue but a lower profit margin
- The technology category is the most profitable
- High discounts significantly reduce profit
- Q4 shows peak sales across years

## Recommendations
- Optimize discount strategy to improve margins
- Focus on high-profit sub-categories
- Improve logistics in low-performing regions
- Strengthen customer retention programs
